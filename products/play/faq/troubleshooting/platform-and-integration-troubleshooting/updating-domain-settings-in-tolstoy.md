# Updating Domain Settings in Tolstoy

To update the domain settings in Tolstoy, follow these steps:

1. **Log into your Tolstoy account.**
2. **Navigate to the 'Settings' section.**
3. **In the 'Domain' settings, update the URL from the Shopify store link to the main domain.**
4. **Save the changes.**

By following these steps, you can ensure that your domain settings are correctly updated in Tolstoy.
