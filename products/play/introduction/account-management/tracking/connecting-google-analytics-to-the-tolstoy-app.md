## Connecting Google Analytics to the Tolstoy App

To add your Google Tag ID for tracking, follow these steps:

1. Go to your Account Profile.
2. Click **Settings**.
3. Navigate to the **Tracking** tab and paste your Google Analytics 4 tracking ID. This will add global tracking details for all Tolstoys. You can overwrite these settings in each Tolstoyâ€™s individual settings.
