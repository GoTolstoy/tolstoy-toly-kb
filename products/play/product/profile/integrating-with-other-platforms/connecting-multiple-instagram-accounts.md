## Connecting Multiple Instagram Accounts

Yes, you can connect multiple Instagram accounts.

To do this:
1. Go to Library.
2. Select Sources.
3. Hover your mouse over Instagram.
4. Click the three dots.
5. Click '+ Connect'.
