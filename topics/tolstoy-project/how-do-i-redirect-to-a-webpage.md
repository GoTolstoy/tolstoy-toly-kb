## How do I redirect to a Webpage?

In any Tolstoy, you can redirect your audience to a web page after they answer your prompt! To set it up, follow these simple steps:
Create your Tolstoy. Navigate to the part you want to redirect to a webpage, and click on the drop-down button. (screenshot image of this step: https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/890341017/8e0a59f1ab81cfae42197f00/image.png)
Choose Link, and add your URL. (screenshot images of this step: https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/890342431/89db296563579235c430fa98/image.png & https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/642736202/3d9a7a1f614697e6c339a08b/image.png)
Click Save once you're done. (screenshot image of this step: https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/890343627/23cac133c5910fb6a0bbc73d/image.png)
